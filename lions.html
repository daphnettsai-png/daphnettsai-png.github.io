Lions-Med: Disaster Healthcare Access

The Problem

When a disaster hits NYC, everyone looks at the ERs; but the real crisis happens in the “grey zone.” If you’re managing a chronic illness, a disaster means you can suddenly be cut off. Missed checkups. Prescriptions run out. Clinics quietly close their doors. Google Maps and clinic websites don’t know what’s happening in real time. What was once manageable can quickly become dangerous.

The Solution

A community-driven platform that provides real-time updates on clinic availability.

Use Case

Users can check whether clinics are open and avoid wasted trips.
